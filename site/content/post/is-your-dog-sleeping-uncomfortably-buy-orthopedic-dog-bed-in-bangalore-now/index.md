## Why Your Dog's Sleep Matters More Than You Think

Dogs sleep between 12 to 14 hours a day. That is more than half their life spent resting. During this time, their muscles recover, joints heal, and energy is restored. A bad sleeping surface can lead to:

* Joint pain and stiffness, especially in older dogs
* Worsening of arthritis in dogs already suffering from it
* Poor posture over time
* Restless sleep that leaves your dog tired and irritable
* Hip dysplasia complications in larger breeds

A regular flat bed or sleeping on the floor simply does not provide the support a dog's body needs especially as they age.

## What Is an Orthopedic Dog Bed?

An orthopedic dog bed is specially designed to support your dog's joints, spine, and muscles while they sleep. It is typically made with high-density memory foam or supportive foam layers that distribute your dog's body weight evenly. This reduces pressure on sensitive joints and allows for deeper, more restful sleep.

Orthopedic beds are especially recommended for:

* Senior dogs
* Dogs with arthritis or hip dysplasia
* Large and giant breed dogs
* Dogs recovering from surgery or injury
* Any dog that simply deserves the best comfort
